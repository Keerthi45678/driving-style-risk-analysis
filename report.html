Driving Style Risk Analysis

Data Pulse: Precision Telematics Clustering

Project Overview

Traditional insurance premiums rely on static demographics like age or location. This project introduces a Usage-Based Insurance (UBI) framework that leverages real-time vehicle telemetry. By analyzing how a driver actually handles the road—specifically through acceleration gradients and braking intensity—we can create a fairer, data-driven pricing model that rewards safety.

01. Data Ingestion

Extracted 100 high-frequency telemetry samples including max acceleration and jerk gradients.

02. Feature Engineering

Engineered "Jerk Accel" and "Aggression Scores" to capture erratic pedal behavior beyond simple speed.

03. Unsupervised ML

Applied K-Means clustering validated by Elbow scores to identify hidden behavioral segments.

04. Risk Mapping

Correlated clusters to risk indices, enabling dynamic premium multipliers and financial tiers.
